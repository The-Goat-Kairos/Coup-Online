Display
- Cards Hidden to Everyone until Card Lost
- Coins

- Choose an action
    - Income
    - Foreign Aid
    - Coup

Character Actions
- Duke (Tax)
- Assassin (Assassinate)
- Captain (Steal)
- Ambassador (Exchange)

Counteractions
- Duke (Counteract Foreign Aid
- Contessa (Counteract Assassination)
- Ambassador/Captain (Counteract Steal)


Gameplay:
Player chooses an action.
If a player has >= 10 coins, force Coup action.
The victim can then choose to counteract with a character they can actually have or not

Then show everyone a screen where they can challenge a previously done character action
Challenge makes player show they had the required influence.
    - If they do, they discard that card and take a random new card from the deck and the other person loses an influence
    - If they lose, they lose an influence and the action is cancelled

Display:
Screen holding your own cards (Bordered and with ratio height:width = 1.5:1)
Screen holding every players coins and cards (Unturned if influence hasn't been lost, otherwise show card)
Every action, counteraction and challenge in a pop up maybe
